Naruto is one of the most popular Japanese manga series, one that was written and illustrated by the legendary Masashi Kishimoto. It was published by Shueisha and it ran from 2000 to 2015, with Naruto Part I containing 27 volumes and with Naruto Part II containing 45 volumes. All 72 volumes have been released in English as well, which helps explain why Naruto is the best anime in terms of its reach and popularity. As for what type of anime is Naruto, it was officially described as an adventure fantasy comedy focused on martial arts. There was also a Naruto anime TV series, with the one titled Naruto airing from 2002 to 2007 and the one titled Naruto: Shippuden running from 2007 to 2017. The latter, the Naruto: Shippuden series, was especially popular and there are still many fans of it, even though the last episode aired over five years ago.
